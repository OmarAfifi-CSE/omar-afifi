Overview

Batrina is not just another shopping app; it's a revolutionary social e-commerce platform that transforms online fashion shopping into a community-driven experience. Built with Flutter and Firebase, it merges commerce with community by allowing users to chat with real buyers for genuine feedback.

From a live product matrix (Color/Size) to seamless deep linking and a powerful Admin Dashboard, Batrina represents the future of how people shop online.

Technical Deep Dive

Building Batrina was an advanced journey into complex app architecture and backend logic. Key technical milestones included:

  • Advanced State Management: Utilizing Cubit & Provider to handle complex global states like Cart, User Profile, and Themes.
  • Complex Business Logic: Implementing a multi-dimensional inventory system (Matrix) where stock depends on both Color and Size variants.
  • Firebase Transactions: Ensuring data integrity during high-concurrency situations (e.g., two users buying the last item).
  • Deep Linking Architecture: Configuring intent filters and handling incoming links for a frictionless "Click-to-Open" experience.
  • Scalable Architecture: Designing a codebase that supports immediate switching between User and Admin modes dynamically.
  • Performance Optimization: Achieving 60 FPS through image caching, lazy loading, and efficient widget rebuilds.
